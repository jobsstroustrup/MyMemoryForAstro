# David Romps

## 中文版

### 基本信息

- **姓名**: David Romps
- **机构**: University of California, Berkeley — Department of Earth and Planetary Science (EPS)
- **公开主页**: https://eps.berkeley.edu/people/david-romps
- **公开身份**: UC Berkeley EPS 教授；研究方向为大气对流物理与气候动力学

### 研究领域

David Romps 的研究聚焦于大气物理与气候建模的数学基础，公开研究方向包括：

- **大气对流物理（Atmospheric convection）**: 湿对流过程的理论分析与高分辨率数值模拟
- **闪电与极端事件（Lightning & extreme events）**: 雷电频率与气候变化的耦合关系
- **羽流大涡模拟（Plume LES）**: 高分辨率大涡模拟在烟羽与对流羽流中的应用

### 与 Zhenyu 的关联

Zhenyu 在 2022–2025 年于 UC Berkeley EPS 与 David Romps 课题组合作，工作内容围绕大气物理与羽流大涡模拟（plume LES）方向展开。该合作的研究主线包括三维 LES 烟羽动力学的数值实现，以及与之相关的诊断方法学。

## English Version

### Basic Info

- **Name**: David Romps
- **Affiliation**: University of California, Berkeley — Department of Earth and Planetary Science (EPS)
- **Public faculty page**: https://eps.berkeley.edu/people/david-romps
- **Public credential**: Professor at UC Berkeley EPS, working on atmospheric convection physics and climate dynamics

### Research Focus

David Romps' research centers on the mathematical foundations of atmospheric physics and climate modeling, with public lines of work including:

- **Atmospheric convection**: theoretical analysis and high-resolution numerical simulation of moist convective processes
- **Lightning & extreme events**: the coupling between lightning frequency and climate change
- **Plume LES**: applications of high-resolution large-eddy simulation to smoke and convective plumes

### Connection to Zhenyu

Zhenyu collaborated with David Romps' group at UC Berkeley EPS during 2022–2025 on atmospheric physics and plume large-eddy simulation (plume LES). The collaboration centered on the numerical implementation of three-dimensional plume dynamics and the associated diagnostic methodology.
