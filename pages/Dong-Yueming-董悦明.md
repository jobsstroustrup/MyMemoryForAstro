## English Version

### Basic Info

- **Name**: Dong Yueming (董悦明)
- **Affiliation (during collaboration)**: Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, School of Physics, Peking University
- **Role**: Lab senior member in Prof. Jing Li's group at PKU during my undergraduate senior-thesis aerosol-retrieval research
- **Direction (collaboration topic)**: Satellite remote-sensing data acquisition workflow / MODIS L1B data download and preprocessing methodology

### Collaboration Summary

I collaborated with Dong Yueming inside Prof. Jing Li's PKU group during my undergraduate senior-thesis aerosol-retrieval research. The specific contribution centered on MODIS L1B data download and processing methodology — covering LAADS DAAC registration, APP Key acquisition, and the wget batch-download workflow as a full onboarding tutorial. This methodological material served as my entry path into the MODIS data pipeline at that time, and it directly underpinned my subsequent dataset-selection (MOD021KM / MYD021KM) and processing decisions.

### Research Direction

Dong Yueming's work during the collaboration focused on standardized procedures for acquiring and preprocessing satellite remote-sensing L1B data within the PKU atmospheric and oceanic sciences setting, forming part of the group's data-operations baseline.
