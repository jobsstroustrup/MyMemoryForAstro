# Inez Fung

## 中文版

### 基本信息

- **姓名**: Inez Fung
- **机构**: University of California, Berkeley — Department of Earth and Planetary Science (EPS)
- **公开主页**: https://eps.berkeley.edu/people/inez-fung
- **公开身份**: 政府间气候变化专门委员会（IPCC）的奠基科学家之一；著名气候科学家

### 研究领域

Inez Fung 长期从事气候科学研究，涵盖以下方向：

- **碳循环（Carbon cycle）**: 全球大气–陆地–海洋之间碳通量的建模与诊断
- **甲烷（CH₄）卫星制图**: 基于卫星观测的温室气体源识别与量化
- **气候建模（Climate modeling）**: 大气化学–气候耦合模型与地球系统模拟

### 与 Zhenyu 的关联

Zhenyu 在 2023–2024 年期间与 Inez Fung 在 UC Berkeley 进行了甲烷 MRV（measurement, reporting, verification）方向的合作，工作内容包括基于 Sentinel-5P/TROPOMI 卫星观测的区域甲烷浓度异常制图，以及自下而上排放清单与自上而下卫星反演的交叉验证方法。

## English Version

### Basic Info

- **Name**: Inez Fung
- **Affiliation**: University of California, Berkeley — Department of Earth and Planetary Science (EPS)
- **Public faculty page**: https://eps.berkeley.edu/people/inez-fung
- **Public credential**: Founding scientist of the IPCC; widely recognized climate scientist

### Research Focus

Inez Fung's research spans several areas of climate science:

- **Carbon cycle**: modeling and diagnosing global carbon fluxes among the atmosphere, land, and ocean
- **Methane (CH₄) satellite mapping**: identifying and quantifying greenhouse-gas sources from space-based observations
- **Climate modeling**: coupled atmospheric-chemistry climate models and Earth-system simulations

### Connection to Zhenyu

Zhenyu collaborated with Inez Fung at UC Berkeley during 2023–2024 on methane MRV (measurement, reporting, verification) work. The collaboration centered on regional methane anomaly mapping using Sentinel-5P / TROPOMI satellite observations, and on cross-validation methods between bottom-up emissions inventories and top-down satellite retrievals.
