# Li Chong · 李充

## 中文版

**李充**(Li Chong)在我于北京大学物理学院开展本科毕业论文(气溶胶反演方向)期间,是 PKU 物理学院 大气与海洋科学系的资深实验室成员,研究方向为 MODIS 被动遥感、气溶胶卫星反演,以及卫星-地基联合反演方法。

我于 2021 至 2022 年期间在 PKU 实验室与李充就气溶胶反演方法进行了合作,内容涉及 MODIS 反演流程的代码协作、反演不确定性的物理诊断,以及对气溶胶垂直分布参数(标高)反演敏感性的方法学讨论。

李充作为 Li C. et al. 2020 *Remote Sensing* 论文的第一作者,与 Caltech Yuk Yung 教授形成了共同作者关系,这一合作链条也是我在本科阶段同时接触 PKU 气溶胶研究方向与 Caltech DSCOVR 大气反演方向的方法学背景之一。

## English Version

**Li Chong** was a senior lab member at the Department of Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences, School of Physics, Peking University, during my undergraduate senior thesis work on aerosol retrieval. Her research covers MODIS passive remote sensing, satellite aerosol retrieval, and joint satellite-ground-based retrieval methods.

I collaborated with Li Chong on aerosol research at PKU during 2021-2022, including the MODIS retrieval code pipeline, physical diagnostics of retrieval uncertainty, and methodological discussions on the sensitivity of retrievals to aerosol vertical-distribution parameters (scale height).

As first author of Li C. et al. 2020 *Remote Sensing*, Li Chong shares co-authorship with Prof. Yuk Yung at Caltech. This co-authorship link is part of the methodological context surrounding my concurrent undergraduate exposure to PKU aerosol retrieval and Caltech DSCOVR atmospheric retrieval.
