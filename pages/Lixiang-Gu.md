# Lixiang Gu · 顾理想

## 中文版

### 基本信息

- **姓名**: 顾理想 (Lixiang Gu)
- **机构**: 北京大学（DSCOVR 单像素地球反演课题组同事；与胡永云、Caltech Yuk Yung、UC Riverside King-Fai Li 一同合作的一份论文草稿的合著者）
- **研究方向**: 反问题与贝叶斯反演方法学；DSCOVR 单像素 Earth-as-an-exoplanet 反演

### 合作概要

我于 2021–2022 年期间在 DSCOVR Bayesian 反演课题组与顾理想有过合作。我提出并整理的 Hardened Balancing Principle (HBP) 正则化判据，最初是针对 PC2（陆/海对比模态）写出的；顾理想随后将这套判据**扩展应用到 PC1（低云模态）与 PC4（高云模态）的反演**上，构成同一组合作者之间的方法学下游延展。

合作产物是一份草稿论文 *"An automatic pipeline for retrieval from DSCOVR data"*（草稿，未公开发表），署名顺序为 Lixiang Gu, Zhenyu He, Siteng Fan*（通讯）, King-Fai Li, Yuk L. Yung, Yongyun Hu。

### 研究方向

反问题正则化方法在卫星单像素遥感反演中的应用；将 HBP / L-curve / GCV / Bayesian 等多种判据并列对比，识别各自的失效模式。

## English Version

### Basic Info

- **Name**: Lixiang Gu (顾理想)
- **Affiliation**: Peking University (colleague in the DSCOVR single-pixel Earth-retrieval collaboration; co-author on a paper draft together with Yongyun Hu, Caltech's Yuk Yung, and UC Riverside's King-Fai Li)
- **Research areas**: inverse-problem and Bayesian inversion methodology; DSCOVR single-pixel "Earth-as-an-exoplanet" retrievals

### Collaboration Summary

I collaborated with Lixiang Gu in the DSCOVR Bayesian retrieval group during 2021–2022. The Hardened Balancing Principle (HBP) regularization criterion that I formulated and wrote up was initially developed for PC2 (the land/ocean contrast mode); Lixiang Gu subsequently **extended the same criterion to retrievals on PC1 (low-cloud mode) and PC4 (high-cloud mode)**, a downstream methodological extension within the same group of co-authors.

The collaboration produced a draft paper, *"An automatic pipeline for retrieval from DSCOVR data"* (draft, not publicly published), with authorship order Lixiang Gu, Zhenyu He, Siteng Fan* (corresponding), King-Fai Li, Yuk L. Yung, Yongyun Hu.

### Research Direction

Application of inverse-problem regularization methods to satellite single-pixel remote-sensing retrievals; side-by-side comparison of HBP, L-curve, GCV, and Bayesian criteria to characterize the failure modes of each.
