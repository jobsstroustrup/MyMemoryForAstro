# Walker/Hadley Circulation Response Under Global Warming @ PKU

## 中文版

### 基本信息
- **时间**: 2019.10 — 2020.05 (~7 months, 本科大二秋季到大二末)
- **角色**: 本科研究 (Ji Nie 组 first project — 本科生科研 onboarding)
- **组织**: 北京大学物理学院大气与海洋科学系
- **指导教师**: [[Ji-Nie-聂绩]] (primary PI)
- **地点**: 北京 / Tianhe-II NSCC-GZ (早期 (undergraduate Tianhe-II account) account)
- **资助**: PKU 本科生科研训练项目 (Zhenyu 在 4 advisor candidate 中 selected Ji Nie climate dynamics direction 之后 onboarding)

### 核心问题
全球变暖 (不同 CO₂ level: Picontrol vs Abrupt2xCO₂ vs Abrupt4xCO₂) 下 Walker Cell 与 Hadley Cell 强度变化 + 两者对热带地区 total mass flux change 的 contribution 分解。

### 🌟 Signature Contribution: Earliest Multi-Method Comparison (Walker strength 3 metric 并列对比)

**2019-11-04 first long report** (`the impact on the strength of tropical circulations 2.pptx`, 43 slides) — Zhenyu 大二 Ji Nie 组会 first long PPT report, 详见 [[2019-First-Long-Report]]。

**Walker strength 量化 method comparison (3 methods 并列对比)**:
1. **Velocity potential @ 200 hPa peak** (Tanaka et al. 2004 method)
2. **Integral (vertical velocity + zonal wind 综合, 选 longitude-height domain 120E-60W × 925-150 hPa, 3 latitudinal zones)** ⭐ — 后被 Walker paper v6 §3.1 Walker 强度 metric inherit 的早期 source
3. **Precipitation + 850 hPa wind velocity vector**

**5 篇 papers 对比表** (Walker 增强 vs 减弱 trend contradiction):
- Tanaka et al. 2004 (velocity potential decomposition method)
- Strengthening of Walker Circulation under Global Warming in an Aqua-Planet (MRI Japan, 3 cumulus parameterization YS/AS/KF parallel)
- CMIP5 projected 减弱 papers
- NCEP/NCAR obs records
- Holton 2004 *An Introduction to Dynamic Meteorology* 教材 reference

**每个 paper 给出 advantage + defect review** (*"the measuring method adopted by the author contradicts to some other scholars' results, the author doesn't explain Table 2 in detail"*) — **multi-method critical comparison** alongside paper review capability 在大二 11 月 first long report 已成形, 这是 **[[Multi-Method-Comparison-Spine]] root evidence** (比 PhD Phase 4.5 SWTG failed cancellation 早 6 年, 比 Walker paper v6 ω-vs-u 早 1.5 年, 比 Caltech DSCOVR 7-method regularization comparison 早 2 年)。

### 2020-09 全员大组会 Walker metric source-of-truth evidence

**`聂绩202009刚开学不久的全员大组会_我的报告 Abrupt4xCo2 vs Picontrol.pptx`** (16 slides, 大三秋季 Ji Nie 组全员大组会):

- All data averaged on **140th-150th year**
- **Hadley strength = max mass-streamfunction**: Picontrol 7.9097e+10 (600 hPa, 13.66°N) / 2xCO₂ 8.807e+10 (12.7°N) / 4xCO₂ 8.4451e+10 (10.84°N, **peak shifts equatorward**)
- **Walker strength = ω@500mb WEP-EEP diff** (WEP 10N-10S × 100E-150E, EEP 10N-10S × 180-100W): Picontrol 0.0913 / 2xCO₂ 0.0587 / 4xCO₂ 0.0909 (monotonic non-trend) ⭐

→ 此 metric 定义 **与 Walker paper v6 §3.1 完全 identical**, 比 paper v6 (2021-10-22) 早 **1 年 7 个月** articulated。Walker paper metric 不是 paper drafting 阶段才决定的, 而是 Phase 0 reading + ENSO 文献 review 阶段已 settle — **大三秋季 research taste 的 early locked-in**。

### Core Problem

**Walker strength quantification — 3-method comparison**:
1. **Velocity potential @ 200 hPa peak** (Tanaka et al. 2004 method)
2. **Integral (combined vertical velocity + zonal wind, longitude-height domain 120E-60W × 925-150 hPa, 3 latitudinal zones)** — later inherited as the Walker-paper-v6 §3.1 Walker-strength metric source
3. **Precipitation + 850 hPa wind velocity vector**

**5-paper comparison table** (Walker strengthening vs weakening trend contradiction):
- Tanaka et al. 2004 (velocity potential decomposition method)
- Strengthening of Walker Circulation under Global Warming in an Aqua-Planet (MRI Japan, 3 cumulus parameterizations YS/AS/KF in parallel)
- CMIP5 projected weakening papers
- NCEP/NCAR observational records

**Each paper is given an advantage + defect review** (*"the measuring method adopted by the author contradicts to some other scholars' results, the author doesn't explain Table 2 in detail"*) — **multi-method critical comparison alongside paper-review capability** was already crystallized in the sophomore-November first long report. This is **[[Multi-Method-Comparison-Spine]] root evidence** (6 years before PhD Phase 4.5 SWTG failed cancellation, 1.5 years before Walker-paper-v6 ω-vs-u, 2 years before Caltech DSCOVR's 7-method regularization comparison).
