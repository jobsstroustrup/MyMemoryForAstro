### 描述
- Sentinel-5P/TROPOMI L2 产品处理（CH4）
- MODIS 与 CALIPSO 联合反演
- DSCOVR 单点光曲线数据处理
- 数据格式：netCDF、HDF5
- 空间数据处理：QA 过滤、空间重映射、KD-tree 索引、网格化聚合
- 与地面排放清单（如 EPA）的对标验证

### 🌟 研究级 signature capabilities

**MODIS+CALIPSO joint retrieval signatures (本科毕业论文, 2021-2022)**
- **First iteratively-coupled MODIS+CALIPSO algorithm** (不是 single-satellite 或 ad-hoc combination)
- **CALIPSO L2 non-physical bug catch + 修正**: -10⁴ km⁻¹ negatives → 修正到 0-0.2 km⁻¹ 合理范围
- **4 Beijing case study**: absolute AOD error ↓ 0.011-0.032, relative ↓ 3-20% (vs single-satellite baseline)
- **Lab scaffolding** under [[Jing-Li-李婧]] with collaborator support on Fernald math, MODIS, and tutorials, plus Caltech bridge via [[Yuk-Yung]]

**DSCOVR single-point Earth retrieval signatures (本科, 2021-2022)**
- **L1 → L2 光曲线 time series processing** (remote workstation bootstrap)
- **Inverse problem regularization** (4 methods compared, 见 [[MCMC-贝叶斯反演]] + Caltech 4 贡献 in [[Caltech-DSCOVR-Research]])

**TROPOMI methane signatures (PhD, 2023-2024)**
- **Vernier caliper aggregated precision discovery**: `np.unique(lats/lons)` 9.76e-5° ≈ 10.87m effective precision (raw ~5.5×3.5 km pixel)
- **Negative-slope regression inversion diagnosis + density+quantile reformulation** (Nebraska R²=0.633)
- **Texas puzzle resolved via free-roaming hypothesis (advisor input from [[Inez-Fung]])**
