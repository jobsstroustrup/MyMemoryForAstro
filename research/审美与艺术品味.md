# 审美与艺术品味 (Aesthetic Sensibility & Artistic Taste)

## 中文版

### 熟练度
熟练

### 描述

理科和工程背景之外的兴趣面——对古典音乐、视觉编排、产品细节的长期关注与收藏式聆听/阅读。

**古典音乐**
- 长期聆听贝多芬重要奏鸣曲：《热情》Op.57、《悲怆》Op.13、《月光》Op.27 No.2 等
- 偏爱 20 世纪德奥学派演绎的权威录音：Wilhelm Kempff（Op.57 慢板的句法呼吸）、Rudolf Serkin（Op.13 / Op.27 的结构化处理）
- 在个人网站中精心策划音乐播放器的曲目，作为站点叙事的组成部分

**视觉美学**
- 个人网站（[[Personal-Website-jobsstroustrup]]）策划了 Portraits 和 Sceneries 两个摄影画廊
- 10+ 张精选背景 banner 图
- 集成樱花飘落、烟花、雪花等动画特效作为审美元素

**产品美学取向**
- 网站命名 "Jobs Stroustrup" — 致敬史蒂夫·乔布斯（产品美学）和 Bjarne Stroustrup（C++ 之父）的双重隐喻
- 主题选择偏好 Material Design（Google 的严谨美学体系），新版网站延续 R8 vanilla CSS + cascade layers 路线（research 古典 serif / blog Material / portfolio Stripe minimal 三套互斥 palette）

**跨学科收藏视角**
- 古典音乐演绎史的版本学习惯与科学论文的版本对照阅读、产品迭代的设计版本回顾，是同一种"收藏-比对-注释"的工作方式
- 写作面：博客与笔记跨技术与人文两侧，但呈现方式偏向叙事而非清单

### 在哪些经历中用到

- [[Personal-Website-jobsstroustrup]] — 个人网站的音乐选择和视觉设计
- early-stage embodied-AI data company (passive shareholder) — BP/Deck 的设计、叙事打磨、对产品细节的关注

### 为什么记录

记录这一面，并不是为了把"品味"作为可量化技能来主张，而是为了在网站与简历的视觉/编排细节背后留下一条说明：选择 serif 还是 Material、选择 Kempff 还是 Serkin、选择 Portraits/Sceneries 而不是单一 hero 图，这些都不是默认值，而是有依据的。这条说明本身比任何"我有审美"的断言都更接近事实。

## English Version

### Proficiency
Proficient

### Description

An interest area beyond the STEM core — sustained attention to classical music, visual composition, and product detail, in a collector's listen/read posture.

**Classical Music**
- Long-running listener of Beethoven's major sonatas: *Appassionata* Op.57, *Pathétique* Op.13, *Moonlight* Op.27 No.2
- Preferred recordings come from the 20th-century German-Austrian tradition: Wilhelm Kempff (the breathing of the Op.57 slow movement) and Rudolf Serkin (the structural reading of Op.13 / Op.27)
- Curates the personal-website music player as part of the site's narrative

**Visual Aesthetics**
- Personal website ([[Personal-Website-jobsstroustrup]]) curates Portraits and Sceneries photography galleries
- 10+ carefully selected banner images
- Integrated cherry blossom, fireworks, snowflake animations as aesthetic elements

**Product Aesthetic Orientation**
- Website name "Jobs Stroustrup" — double homage to Steve Jobs (product aesthetics) and Bjarne Stroustrup (C++ creator)
- Theme preference: Material Design (Google's rigorous aesthetic system); the rebuild continues this lineage via the R8 vanilla-CSS + cascade-layers approach (three mutually exclusive per-collection palettes: research classical serif / blog Material / portfolio Stripe minimal)

**Cross-domain collector's view**
- Comparing recordings of a Beethoven sonata, comparing revisions of a scientific manuscript, and comparing design iterations of a product page are, in practice, the same "collect–compare–annotate" routine
- Writing surface: notes and posts span technical and reflective topics, but the rendering style favors narrative over enumeration

### Used In

- [[Personal-Website-jobsstroustrup]] — music curation and visual design
- early-stage embodied-AI data company (passive shareholder) — BP/Deck design, narrative polish, attention to product detail

### Why It Is Recorded

This page is not a claim that "taste" is a quantifiable skill. It is a footnote behind the visual and editorial choices on the site and résumé: choosing serif over Material, choosing Kempff over another reading, choosing Portraits/Sceneries over a single hero image — these are not defaults, they are reasoned. That footnote is closer to the truth than any assertion of taste itself would be.
